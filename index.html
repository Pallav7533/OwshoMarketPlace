About Owsho

India's First Hyperlocal Multi-Vendor Marketplace


Owsho is India's first Zero-Commision Based hyperlocal multi-vendor marketplace that connects nearby customers with local businesses and service providers – all in one app. From daily essentials and groceries to electronics, fashion, and home services, Owsho brings everything from nearby shops to your doorstep in just minutes.

We empower local shops and service professionals to go digital, grow their business, and reach more customers without any commission cut. We connect your neighborhood stores and service experts directly with customers. Our platform is designed to help every vendor – small or big – sell products and services both within the city (quick delivery) and across India (e-commerce model).

Whether you're a vendor looking to grow online, or a customer seeking instant, reliable, and your offline market online shopping – Owsho is your go-to solution.

Owsho isn't just a marketplace — it's a movement.

We're on a mission to bring India's 1 crore+ offline shops online — with speed, dignity, and complete ownership.

Join us — and be part of India's retail shop digitally.

How Owsho Works

1

Explore Trusted Local Shops & Services

From groceries and electronics to AC repair and salon bookings — discover 1000s of verified vendors near you. All available on one powerful platform.

2

Place Your Order in Minutes

Choose your products or book a service, select your preferred time, and place the order — all in just a few taps. No hassle, no waiting.

3

Superfast Delivery from Your Neighbourhood

Our dedicated delivery fleet picks up your order directly from nearby vendors and delivers it to your doorstep in just minutes.

4

Full Earnings, No Middlemen

Keep every rupee you earn. Owsho eliminates middlemen, so vendors get complete control and 100% profit — with no commission cuts, ever.

5

Seamless Digital Storefronts for Every Vendor

Every vendor, big or small, gets a powerful online presence with built-in tools for inventory, orders, and customer engagement — no technical skills needed.

6

Real-Time Tracking & Reliable Support

Get live updates, instant support, and full transparency at every step. Owsho ensures a smooth, secure, and satisfying experience — every single time.
